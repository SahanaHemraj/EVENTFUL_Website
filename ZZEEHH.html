About

Unacademy

Unacademy is an Indian online learning platform that offers a wide range of courses and lessons taught by experienced educators. It was founded in 2015 and has since become one of the largest online learning platforms in India. Unacademy offers courses in various categories, including UPSC, SSC, banking, railways, CAT, JEE, NEET, GATE, and more.

In addition, the platform also provides free and paid classes on various skills and hobbies, such as programming, photography, music, and cooking. Unacademy offers live classes, recorded video lessons, quizzes, and practice tests to help learners improve their skills and prepare for competitive exams. The platform is accessible through its website and mobile app, which has over 50 million downloads.
